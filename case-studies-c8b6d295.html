Multi-City Market Entry — Gulf-Based Conglomerate
A Gulf-based conglomerate with an established regional presence sought to enter Pakistan's retail and logistics sectors. They lacked regulatory intelligence, distribution infrastructure, and the commercial relationships required for successful entry. Investo conducted a full market readiness assessment, structured the regulatory clearance strategy, identified and negotiated with distribution and logistics partners across Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, and supported the commercial launch across all three cities simultaneously — from first advisory engagement to operational launch in under nine months.

Investment Management · Family Office · Karachi
Private Portfolio Construction — Pakistan Family Office
A Karachi-based family office with significant liquidity following a business exit engaged Investo to design and structure a formal investment portfolio for the first time. They had previously managed assets informally and required a disciplined framework for capital allocation, risk management, and governance. Investo developed an investment policy statement, designed an asset allocation framework across real estate, technology, and manufacturing, sourced and evaluated specific investment opportunities, and established ongoing portfolio monitoring infrastructure — giving the family office the governance standards of a professional institutional investor.

Within twelve months of launch, the brand had activated four marketplace channels and initiated cross-border commerce into two Gulf markets.
